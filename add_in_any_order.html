Add in Any Order (Commutative Property)

Summary

The commutative property tells us that numbers can be added in any order and still give the same result. For example: 4 + 5 = 5 + 4 = 9

Examples

  • 3 + 6 = 9 and 6 + 3 = 9
  • 2 + 8 = 10 and 8 + 2 = 10
  • 5 + 4 = 9 and 4 + 5 = 9
