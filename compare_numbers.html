Compare Numbers Using >, <, =

Summary

When comparing two numbers, we use symbols:

  • > means greater than
  • < means less than
  • = means equal to

Examples

  • 9 < 15 ‚Üí 9 is less than 15
  • 22 > 10 ‚Üí 22 is greater than 10
  • 7 = 7 ‚Üí both are equal
