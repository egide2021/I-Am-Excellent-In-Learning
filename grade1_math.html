Grade 1 Math

Explore foundational math strands and topics for Grade 1 learners.

1. Numbers and Number System

  • Count to 10 and 20
  • Identify and write numbers
  • Compare numbers (more/less)
  • Place value: Ones and Tens
  • Skip counting by 2s and 5s

2. Addition and Subtraction

  • Addition strategies up to 10
  • Add in any order
  • Subtraction within 10
  • Complete the addition sentence to make ten

3. Measurement

  • Compare lengths and weights
  • Use non-standard units

4. Geometry and Spatial Sense

  • Identify shapes
  • Sort and classify shapes

5. Patterns and Algebraic Thinking

  • Recognize and extend patterns
