🔺 Identify and Name 2D Shapes

📝 Summary:

  • Circle – no sides or corners
  • Square – 4 equal sides and 4 corners
  • Triangle – 3 sides and 3 corners
  • Rectangle – 4 sides and 4 corners (2 long, 2 short)
