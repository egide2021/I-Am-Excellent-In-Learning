📏 Use Number Lines and Number Bonds

Summary: Use number lines to count steps, and number bonds to break numbers into parts to help with addition.

Examples:

  • Number Line: 3 + 2 → Start at 3, jump 2 steps → Answer: 5
  • Number Bond: 4 + 5 → Break 5 into 1 + 4 → (4 + 4) + 1 = 9
