🔢 Understand Place Value (Tens and Ones)

Summary: Break apart numbers into tens and ones to understand how they are built. For example, the number 42 is made of 4 tens and 2 ones.

Examples:

  • 🟦 4 tens and 🟨 2 ones → Answer: 42
  • 🟦 7 tens and 🟨 9 ones → Answer: 79
  • 🟦 1 ten and 🟨 3 ones → Answer: 13
