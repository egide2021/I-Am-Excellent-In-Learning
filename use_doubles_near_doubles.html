Use Doubles and Near Doubles

Summary

Doubles are when you add the same number twice (like 4 + 4). Near doubles are one more or less than a double (like 4 + 5).

Examples

  • 4 + 4 = 8 (Double)
  • 4 + 5 = 9 (Near double)
  • 6 + 6 = 12 (Double)
  • 6 + 5 = 11 (Near double)
