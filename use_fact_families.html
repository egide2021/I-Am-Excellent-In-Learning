🧩 Use Fact Families

Summary: Fact families help you see how addition and subtraction are connected. If 3 + 4 = 7, then 7 − 3 = 4 and 7 − 4 = 3.

Examples:

  • Fact Family for 3, 4, and 7: 3 + 4 = 7, 4 + 3 = 7, 7 − 3 = 4, 7 − 4 = 3
  • If you know 5 + 2 = 7, then you also know 7 − 5 = 2 and 7 − 2 = 5
